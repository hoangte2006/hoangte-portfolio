## Overview

This was a small and fun summer project with the goal of creating a "gun" that could catch a water bottle using compressed air. The entire product was built from recycled materials, an old bicycle pump, and a few simple mechanical components.

## Build Process

-   **Ideation:** Designing a clamp mechanism that could open and close using air pressure.
-   **Fabrication:** The parts were cut, ground, and assembled by hand, requiring precision and patience to ensure the mechanism operated smoothly.
-   **Pneumatic System:** Utilized an old bicycle pump as the compressed air source, with a valve and tubing for control.

This project is a testament to how, with a little patience and creativity, we can turn discarded items into interesting and fun toys.
