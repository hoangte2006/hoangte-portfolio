## Pulse Signal Acquisition & Isolation

Captured raw pulses directly from the Pickup Coil (Stator Trigger) going to the CDI/IC unit to ensure a consistent and reliable frequency source.

Integrated a PC817 Optocoupler for galvanic isolation, preventing back-EMF and electrical noise from the engine from damaging the microcontroller.

## Noise Suppression & Signal Integrity

Engineered a filtering stage using ceramic and electrolytic capacitors to smooth out "dirty" pulses, delivering a stabilized signal for accurate RPM calculation.

## Robust Power Management

Built a power regulation module using the LM7805 and a decoupling network to transform unstable 12V vehicle power into a steady 5V supply.

## Embedded Development

Wrote the core processing logic in C/C++ (Arduino-based) to convert trigger pulses into real-time RPM data displayed on a 16x2 LCD.
